MODULE 2
The Demo (Your #1 Weapon)
When you show someone a live demo on your phone, the sale is 80% done. They can SEE what they're getting. No imagination required.

How to Show a Demo

  • Open the demo on YOUR phone — don't hand them the phone
  • Hold it so you're BOTH looking at it together (side by side, tilted toward them)
  • Scroll through slowly — let them absorb each section
  • Say: "This is what a site for YOUR type of business looks like"
  • Point out specific features: "See this contact form? Every inquiry goes straight to your phone"

MODULE 4
When They Push Back
Every objection has a response. Memorize these 6 and you'll never be caught off guard.

The Rule: Agree First, Then Redirect

Never argue. Say "I totally get that" or "That makes sense." Then pivot to the value. People buy from people who listen, not people who push.

❌ "I can't afford it right now."
✅ Say this:

"I totally get that — budget is real. That's actually why we offer payment plans. Half upfront, half when it goes live. And honestly, if your site brings in even ONE new customer, it pays for itself. How much is one new client worth to your business?"

❌ "I need to think about it."
✅ Say this:

"100%, take your time. Can I ask — what specifically are you thinking over? Is it the price, the timing, or something about the site? [Let them answer.] Got it. What if I have Sabino call you and walk through it? He can answer any questions way better than I can. Would that help?"

❌ "I already have a website."
✅ Say this:

"That's great! Can I ask — is it bringing you leads? Does it look good on phones? [Show your demo.] This is what a modern 2026 site looks like. Compare it with yours and see which one you'd rather have representing your business. If yours is better, no hard feelings!"

❌ "My nephew does websites."
✅ Say this:

"That's awesome — family helping family. But real talk — is it showing up on Google? Is it bringing in new customers? Because that's really the difference between a website and a website that makes money. Just look at our demo and compare. No pressure."

❌ "I don't need a website."
✅ Say this:

"I hear you. But when was the last time YOU looked up a business that didn't have a website? What did you think? Your customers are thinking the same thing. The businesses in your area that DO have sites are getting the calls that should be going to you."

❌ "How do I know this is legit?"
✅ Say this:

"Great question. Here — check out our main site. [Show sanchezelevationai.com.] We have real clients with real live sites right now. A construction company, a loan officer, a photographer — all live and working. I can also connect you directly with Sabino, the owner."
