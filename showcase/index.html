▸ The story

Solo. Nineteen. Pico Rivera.

I was nineteen, broke, and watching every "AI agency" YouTuber sell a $5K course on cold email. So I built the agency they were teaching about — and I built it the way an engineer would.

Instead of one chatbot, I built an operating system. 100+ specialist skills. An 8-mode dispatcher that classifies every input and routes it to the right chain. A 5-archetype Council that pressure-tests every money decision. 38 vertical demos that deploy in 30 seconds. A CRM that scans for revenue leaks every 6 hours. Hooks that filter every outbound message through my own voice corpus before it leaves.

RIVVEN runs autonomously, 24/7, on a laptop in my bedroom. While I sleep, cron jobs back up the source, audit deployed sites for drift, scrape industry intel from the people I learn from, and grade tomorrow's quota against today's pace.

This isn't a class project. It's the production stack of a real business with real paying clients. And it was built by one teenager with one Claude subscription.
