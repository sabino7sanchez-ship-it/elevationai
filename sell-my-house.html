Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How fast can you close?
We can close in as little as 14 days. However, we work on YOUR timeline. If you need 30, 60, or even 90 days, that's perfectly fine. You pick the date that works best for you.
Do I need to make repairs before selling?
Absolutely not. We buy houses in any condition — roof damage, foundation issues, mold, outdated everything, you name it. Don't spend a single dollar on repairs. We handle all of that after we buy.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero fees. Zero commissions. We even pay the closing costs. The cash offer we give you is the amount you walk away with. No surprises, no hidden charges.
How do you determine the offer price?
We look at recent comparable sales in your area, the current condition of the property, and necessary repairs. We then make a fair cash offer that reflects the true market value minus our estimated repair and holding costs. We're transparent about how we arrive at our number.
What if I owe more than the house is worth?
We work with homeowners in all types of situations, including those who are underwater on their mortgage. We can explore options like short sales or other creative solutions. Give us a call and we'll walk through your specific situation — no judgment, just solutions.
