Mobile Auto Detailing — Case Study

Industry: Auto Detailing Location: Los Angeles, CA Timeline: 2 Weeks
Zero online presence to inbound leads in 2 weeks

// The Challenge

Word of Mouth Wasn't Enough Anymore

Daniel R. runs a mobile auto detailing business in Los Angeles. His work speaks for itself — flawless paint corrections, ceramic coatings, and interior details that make cars look showroom-new. But there was a problem: he was invisible online.

One hundred percent of his clients came from word of mouth. No website. No way for new customers to search, find, and book him. Meanwhile, competitors doing worse work with better websites were getting all the calls.

  • No website or a completely outdated one
  • Zero online presence — invisible to Google searches
  • No booking system or way to capture leads
  • No quote calculator — pricing handled manually over text
  • Competitors with inferior work ranking above him
  • 100% reliant on word of mouth for new business

// The Solution

AI-Powered Website Built to Convert

We built Daniel a complete digital presence from scratch — not just a website, but a lead-generating machine powered by AI tools, SEO, and conversion-first design.

⚡

AI Quote Calculator

Visitors select vehicle type, service, and add-ons. Price calculates live — no waiting, no back-and-forth.

📅

Online Booking System

Integrated scheduling that lets customers pick a date, time, and service — booked directly to Daniel's calendar.

🔍

Local SEO Setup

Targeted "auto detailing near me" + LA area keywords. Google Business Profile fully optimized and verified.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built for phones first. Fast load times, thumb-friendly navigation, one-tap calls and booking.

📷

Before/After Gallery

High-impact visual gallery showcasing Daniel's best work — paint corrections, ceramic coats, interior details.

⭐

Review Integration

Google Reviews pulled in and displayed on-site for instant social proof and trust signals.

// The Results

From Invisible to Inbound Leads

Within two weeks of launch, Daniel's business went from zero online presence to receiving inbound leads weekly. The AI quote calculator captures leads around the clock — even when Daniel is working on a car.
