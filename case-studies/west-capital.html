// The Challenge

Invisible in a Sea of 500 Reps

Manny Aranda is a loan officer at West Capital Lending. Like every other rep at his company, he was handed the same generic quote tools — identical interfaces, identical branding, zero personal identity. In an industry where trust and first impressions close deals, Manny had no way to stand out.

⚠

Generic Tools

Company-issued quote calculators were cookie-cutter templates shared across hundreds of loan officers. Nothing felt personal or professional.

🚫

No Personal Brand

Manny's name, face, and contact info were buried. Clients couldn't distinguish him from any other rep sending the same links.

📴

Low Engagement

Clients would receive a quote link and never open it. Leads went cold before a conversation even started.

👥

500 Identical Reps

Every loan officer at the company sent the exact same thing. There was zero differentiation in how they presented to prospects.

// The Solution

A Custom AI Quote Tool — Built Overnight

Rivven built Manny a fully custom, AI-powered mortgage quote tool in a single night. Not a template. Not a plugin. A purpose-built system designed to make him look like the most professional loan officer in the room.

  • ✓

    Personalized Branding

    Manny's name, photo, contact info, and NMLS number front and center. Every quote screams professionalism and builds instant trust.

  • ✓

    6 Loan Type Calculations

    Instant rate calculations for FHA, Conventional, VA, USDA, Jumbo, and Non-QM loans. Clients see real numbers in seconds, not days.

  • ✓

    Mobile-Optimized

    Designed mobile-first. Clients can pull up a quote on their phone mid-conversation, at an open house, or from a text link.

  • ✓

    Bilingual EN/ES

    Social Sharing
