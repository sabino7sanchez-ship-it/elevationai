01 — The Challenge

Zero Online Presence in a Bilingual Market

Santiago T. runs Allendes Group Inc, a construction and landscaping company serving a deeply bilingual community across Southern California. Despite delivering quality work on the ground, they had zero digital footprint. No website. No Google presence. Nothing for potential clients to find when searching online.

The challenge ran deeper than just visibility. Their team included two reps — one English-speaking, one Spanish-speaking — and there was no system to route incoming calls to the right person based on the caller's language. Leads were being lost, confused, or handed off awkwardly. The entire bilingual market was going unserved.

  • ✗ No website or digital presence of any kind
  • ✗ Zero Google visibility — potential clients could not find them
  • ✗ Bilingual market (EN/ES) completely unserved online
  • ✗ Two reps speaking different languages with no call routing
02 — The Solution

Full Bilingual System — Built in One Night

We designed and deployed a complete bilingual website with a seamless EN/ES language toggle that let visitors browse the entire site in their preferred language. Every piece of content, every call-to-action, every service description — available in both English and Spanish.

The real power was in the smart call routing system. When a visitor on the English version calls or messages, they reach the English-speaking rep. Spanish visitors connect directly to the Spanish-speaking rep. No confusion, no handoffs, no lost leads.

  • ✓ Full bilingual website with EN/ES language toggle across every page
  • ✓ Dual-rep phone routing — English calls to one rep, Spanish to another
  • ✓ WhatsApp integration for both languages with direct rep routing
  • ✓ GA4 analytics tracking every call, form submission, and user interaction
  • ✓ Complete service gallery with professional project showcase
  • ✓ JSON-LD structured data for enhanced search engine visibility
