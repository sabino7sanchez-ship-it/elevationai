Questions? Answered.

How fast can you actually build something?
Most sites and tools are live within 24–48 hours. I don't do months-long timelines. You tell me what you need, I build it, and you're live. The quote tool for my first client was built and deployed in one night.
Do I need technical knowledge?
Zero. I handle the code, hosting, and deployment. You get a link you can text to clients. If you can send a text message, you can use everything I build.
What industries do you work with?
I've built for mortgage lending, construction, handyman services, and auto detailing. But I work with any small business that needs a professional online presence, lead capture, or a tool that makes their workflow faster.
Can I make changes after it's built?
Yes. On the Growth plan, you get unlimited updates. On Starter, you get 30 days of bug fixes. You're never stuck with something you don't love.
What if I don't like what you build?
I show you a live working preview before you pay anything on Starter. If it's not what you want, you don't pay. That's the deal.
Why work with you instead of a bigger agency?
Because I ship faster, charge less, and care more than any agency you'll find. Big agencies have 50 people between you and the person writing your code. With me, I'm the one on the call and I'm the one building it. You get my best every time.
Is my client data secure?
Everything I build runs on your own hosted page — no third-party databases storing your client info. Quote data is generated on-the-fly and never logged. You control everything.
Do you work with individual LOs or brokerages?
Both. Individual loan officers get their own branded tool. Brokerages get a white-label version their whole team can use. Brokerage pricing is custom — book a call and we'll talk numbers.
What's in the free demo?
A 30-minute call where I show you the live quote tool in action, we talk about what your business specifically needs, and I give you a price and timeline on the spot. No pitch deck. No slide show. Just a real conversation and real answers.
