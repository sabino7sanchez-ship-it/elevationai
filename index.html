// faq

Questions? Answers.

How fast can you build something?
Most tools are built and deployed within 24-48 hours. I don't do months-long timelines. You tell me what you need, I build it, and you're live.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
Zero. I handle everything — the code, the hosting, the deployment. You get a link. That's it. If you can text a link to a client, you can use my tools.
What industries do you work with?
I specialize in mortgage lending and real estate, but my tools work for any industry that needs client-facing pages, calculators, or admin dashboards.
Can I make changes after it's built?
Yes. On the Growth plan, you get unlimited updates. On Starter, you get 30 days of bug fixes. Either way, you're never stuck with something that doesn't work.
Why should I hire a 19-year-old?
Because I ship faster, charge less, and care more than any agency you'll find. I'm building my reputation — which means you get my absolute best work. Every single time.
What if I don't like it?
I show you a working preview before you pay anything on the Starter plan. If it's not what you want, you don't pay. Simple.
Is my client data secure?
Everything I build runs on your own hosted page — no third-party databases storing your client info. Quote data is generated on-the-fly and never logged. You control everything.
Can I show this to my compliance team?
Yes. The tools don't store PII, don't submit data to external servers, and display only the numbers you input. I can also provide documentation on request for compliance review.
Do you build for individual LOs or brokerages?
Both. Individual loan officers get their own branded tool. Brokerages get a white-label version that every agent on the team can use. Brokerage pricing is custom — book a call.
What's included in the free demo?
A live 30-minute call where I show you the existing quote tool, we talk about what you'd change, and I give you a price and timeline on the spot. No pitch deck. No fluff. Just answers.
