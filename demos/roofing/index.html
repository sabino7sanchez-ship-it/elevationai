Our Services

Everything Your Roof Needs

From minor repairs to full commercial installs — Summit handles it all with the same level of precision and warranty protection.

🏗️
New Roof Installation
Full tear-off and installation of new roofing systems. We work with all major materials — asphalt, tile, metal, and flat roofing. Free detailed estimate provided before any work begins.
🛡️ Manufacturer + Labor Warranty
🔧
Roof Repair
Leaks, cracked tiles, missing shingles, flashing failures — we diagnose and repair the root cause, not just the symptoms. Same-day and next-day repairs available.
🛡️ 2-Year Repair Warranty
🔄
Re-Roofing
Extend the life of your existing structure with a professional re-roof overlay. Cost-effective alternative to full replacement when the deck is sound. Save up to 30% vs. full tear-off.
🛡️ 15-Year Workmanship Warranty
🏢
Flat Roof Systems
TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen flat roofing for commercial and residential low-slope applications. Energy-efficient white membrane options available. Ideal for additions and garages.
🛡️ 20-Year System Warranty
💧
Gutter Installation
Seamless aluminum and copper gutters custom-formed on-site. Gutter guards, downspout extensions, and underground drainage solutions. Protects your foundation and landscaping.
🛡️ Lifetime Gutter Warranty

Storm Damage Restoration
Emergency tarping, insurance claim documentation, and full restoration after hail, wind, or fallen tree damage. We work directly with all major insurance carriers. 24/7 emergency response.
