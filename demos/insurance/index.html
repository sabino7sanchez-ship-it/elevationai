About Your Agent

Independent. Local. On Your Side.

Roberto Medina has been serving Orange County families and businesses for over 8 years. As an independent agent, he's not locked to any single carrier — meaning he shops the whole market to find you the best combination of coverage and price.

Born and raised in Southern California, Roberto is bilingual in English and Spanish. He understands the specific needs of OC families — from protecting a first home in Anaheim to insuring a small business in Santa Ana.
