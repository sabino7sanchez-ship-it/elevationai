About Marcus

Your Coach.
Your Results.

Marcus Webb is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer with 5 years of hands-on experience transforming physiques across Los Angeles. What started as his own personal journey from skinny-fat college student to competitive amateur athlete became a calling to help others do the same.

He doesn't believe in cookie-cutter programs or one-size-fits-all nutrition. Every client gets a fully custom plan built around their schedule, body type, training history, and specific goals — and Marcus stays in the trenches with you the entire way.

His specialty? Body recomposition — the art of losing fat and building muscle simultaneously. Forget the old "bulk and cut" cycle. Marcus's clients get leaner and stronger at the same time.
