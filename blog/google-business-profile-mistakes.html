5 Google Business Profile Mistakes Killing Your Local Rankings

I audit local business Google profiles every week. And every week I see the same five mistakes — costing businesses dozens of customers per month without them knowing. Every one takes less than 20 minutes to fix.

Mistake #1: Wrong or Too-Generic Category

The Problem

Your primary category tells Google which searches to show you in. A plumber who picks "Home Services" competes against every home service business. This alone can drop you 10+ positions.

The Fix (2 min)

GBP > Edit profile > Category. Pick the MOST SPECIFIC option. "Plumber" not "Home Services." Add 2-3 secondary categories.

Mistake #2: Not Posting on GBP

The Problem

Google favors active profiles. No posts in months = Google thinks you're inactive. Your competitor who posts weekly looks more relevant to the algorithm.

The Fix (5 min/week)

Post once a week. Project photo, quick tip, seasonal promo. Google Posts show up directly in your listing and signal activity.

Mistake #3: Ignoring Reviews

The Problem

Businesses with 20+ reviews rank significantly higher than those with 3-5. Not responding to reviews tells customers you don't care. Listings with responses get 42% more direction requests.

The Fix (ongoing)

Getting reviews: Text every customer a direct review link within 24 hours of service.
Responding: Reply to EVERY review within 24 hours. Positive: thank them. Negative: apologize and offer to resolve privately.

Mistake #4: Incomplete Profile

The Problem

Missing hours, blank description, no services listed. Incomplete = unprofessional. Google fills gaps with guesses (often wrong). Competitors with complete profiles win.

The Fix (20 min)

Fill in everything: 750-char keyword-rich description, accurate hours, all services listed, website URL, all applicable attributes (veteran-owned, wheelchair accessible, etc.).

Mistake #5: No Photos or Bad Photos

The Problem

Listings with photos get 35% more website clicks and 42% more direction requests. No photos = no clicks. Stock photos don't count.

The Fix (30 min)

Upload 20+ real photos: storefront, interior, team, 10+ work samples, vehicles, happy customers. Add 2-3 new photos monthly.

Quick Self-Audit

Give yourself 1 point for each:

  1. Specific primary category (not generic)
  2. 2+ secondary categories
  3. 750-char description with keywords
  4. Accurate hours including holidays
  5. All services listed
  6. 20+ Google reviews
  7. Every review responded to
  8. Posted on GBP in last 7 days
  9. 20+ real photos
  10. Website URL linked

8-10: Ahead of 90% of local businesses.
5-7: Solid but leaving leads on the table.
0-4: Your competitors are outranking you right now.

Every fix on this list is free. 1-2 hours of work can be the difference between invisible and top of local search.
